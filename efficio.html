Efficio: Designing clarity for the market’s leading procurement consultancy

2024 — 2025

CHALLENGE

Consultants relied on a fragmented ecosystem of disconnected tools, forcing manual data bridging and slowing decisions across complex, multi-client engagements.

STRATEGY

I led end-to-end UX across multiple workstreams within an Agile delivery environment, establishing a continuous discovery loop using Lean UX principles.

RESULTS

Measurable reductions in time-on-task and error rates validated adoption, while accelerating​ time to insight, supporting smarter decisions, and ultimately driving stronger commercial outcomes, increased savings delivery, and resilience.

CHALLENGE

Connected platform

Procurement is a high-stakes discipline built on the intersection of human relationships and data precision. At Efficio, the world’s leading procurement consultancy, the challenge was to move beyond simple digitization toward true architectural unity. The Connected Platform was designed as the firm’s central nervous system: a modern, intelligent ecosystem connecting consultants, clients, and millions of data points through a single, cohesive interface.

STRATEGY

AI Enabled

Procurement consultants often spent more time navigating the tools than acting on insights. Our task was to consolidate data and workflows into a unified platform without disrupting active global projects. This meant balancing innovation with operational stability. Every new pattern had to integrate with existing databases, align with security and compliance requirements, and remain adaptable to evolving users needs.

Supplier Discovery

Reimagined as an intelligent search layer that surfaces suppliers based on performance, compliance, and market relevance. Consultants can now identify optimal partners in seconds rather than hours, using dynamic filters and AI-assisted ranking models.

Workflow Integration

Unified client-side visibility with internal project management, closing the gap between delivery, communication, and reporting. The result is a seamless experience where project data, client updates, and operational tools coexist in one coherent flow.
